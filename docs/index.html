Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

◯

Calendar at a glance

Filled dots are logged days. Dashed dots are predicted. A tiny mark under a date means there is a note saved for it.

◷

Rolling prediction

Predictions improve with history — a rolling average of your most recent cycle lengths, with outliers filtered.

✎

Day notes

Tap any date to log flow (light / medium / heavy), mood, or symptoms. Optional; nothing forced.

◗

Tap to log

Tap a day on the calendar to mark a period start. Tap a day already inside a logged period to remove it.

⊘

Zero network

There is no account, no leaderboard, no cloud sync, and no analytics. The app never makes a network request.

⚙

Tunable defaults

Settings let you set the starting cycle and period length. Once you have history, the app uses your actual averages instead.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Log the first start

Open the app, tap the calendar day your period started. The app records it and begins tracking from there.

02

Let it learn

Log each cycle's start. With two or more cycles, the app shifts from default 28-day prediction to a rolling average of your actual gaps.

03

Add notes when you want

Tap any day for the day-editor sheet. Add flow, mood, symptoms — or nothing.

04

Trust the privacy

The data is never sent anywhere. Uninstalling the app removes everything. There's nothing to subpoena because there's no server.
