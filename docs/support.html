Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Why local-only?

Reproductive health data is a legal-exposure issue in the United States and elsewhere. Quiet Cycle is structured so there is no record outside your phone to subpoena, breach, or be resold by an advertising broker.

How accurate is the prediction?

Predictions improve with more history. With one logged cycle the app falls back to your configured default. With two or more, it averages the most recent six cycle lengths, filtering anything outside 18–40 days as an outlier before averaging.

What happens if I change the default period length?

Already-logged periods are frozen at their original end date, so they don't retroactively change. New logged periods pick up the new default.

Is anything sent anywhere?

No. There is no account, no login, no cloud sync, no server, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Every byte stays on this device until you delete it.

How do I delete everything?

Uninstall the app. Individual periods can be removed by tapping a day inside the period and choosing Remove period. Individual notes can be cleared by emptying the fields in the day editor.

Need help, or want to report a bug?
