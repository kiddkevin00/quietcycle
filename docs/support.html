Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Why does this exist?

The biggest free period trackers run ads, share with advertisers, or store data on their servers. In jurisdictions where reproductive health data has been subpoenaed, that's not theoretical. Quiet Cycle is a deliberate counterweight.

How accurate is the prediction?

With one logged cycle, the app uses your default cycle length. With two or more, it uses a rolling average of your most recent gaps (filtering outliers outside 18–40 days).

Can I sync it across devices?

No. By design — there is no account and no server to sync with. If you switch phones, the data does not transfer.

Can I export my data?

Not yet. Export is on the roadmap. For now, the data is stored locally and uninstalling removes it.

Need help, or want to report a bug?
